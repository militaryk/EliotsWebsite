About us

This website provides an in-depth look at some of my favourite games. These include: Brawlhalla, Minecraft, Town of Salem and Heave Ho.

I will explain the objective of the game, how to play the game, strategies that can make you better at the game and why I like to play the game. I will also explain (if there is one) the backstory, or lore of the game.
